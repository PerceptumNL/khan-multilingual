Setting up a class

To get the most out of Khan Academy, you'll need to set up accounts. We've created a handy guide to make sure you'll be up and running in no time.

We now have our own Khan Academy login, so you can sign up for Khan Academy with any email address. If your students are over age 13, they can set up accounts with Khan Academy by clicking "login" in the upper right-hand corner of the screen, then clicking "create one" under "Login with a Khan Academy account." Students will then be prompted to enter their birthdate and any email address.

Students under age 13 can have their own Khan Academy accounts too! To create an account for your child, make sure you are logged into your own account and go to www.khanacademy.org/createchild. You will remain your child's permanent coach and can manage his or her password. Students under age 13 cannot make their profiles public or post public messages in discussion forums. If you'd like your child to be able to add other coaches (e.g., his or her teacher), click "coach" in the upper right-hand corner of the screen and choose "manage students." Hover next to your child's name and select "allow other coaches." You can find more information about under-13 accounts here.

You can also log into Khan Academy with personal Google and facebook accounts or with Google Apps for Education accounts (most school systems qualify). If you'd like to set up Google Apps for Education accounts to register your class on Khan Academy, you can find more information here:

  • Google Apps for Education common questions

A smooth start to class

To get started easily, we recommend pre-registering students for class with assigned email accounts (e.g., john-appleseed@school.org for the student John Appleseed) and passwords (e.g., school2012). Students can use these email addresses and passwords to log into Khan Academy through the Khan Academy login or the Google login.

Logging in to a new system for the first time can be a bit hectic, so we included a sample handout to guide students through the initial login process.

Creating class lists

You can find all students who have added you as their coach by clicking “Coach” in the upper-right-hand corner of the site and choosing “manage students.” Some teachers like to separate their students into multiple class lists (e.g., one list for each class period they teach). If you’d like to split students into more than one class list,

  • Click “create a new list” on the left-hand side of the screen
  • Name the list (e.g., first period or Algebra) and select "OK"
  • Repeat for as many class lists as you'd like to create

Now you'll want to add students to the class lists you just created. In the center of the screen, you'll see a list of all of your students.

  • Hover your mouse to the right of each student’s name; you’ll see a drop-down menu of all of your class lists appear
  • Click the list to which you’d like to add each student. For example, if you want to add Jane to “Algebra,” hover next to her name and click the box for “Algebra.” You can add students to more than one list if you'd like

You can see all of the real-time reports for all of your students and for each class list you created by clicking on the “all students” drop-down menu on the upper right-hand side of the coach page and selecting the class list you’d like to view.
