Make a Stock Gift

To make physical and electronic stock gifts, please download this form. The donated value of the stock will be calculated by taking the average of the high and low prices for the day the date stamp indicates on the certificate.

Please email us if you intend to transfer stock to our account so we may acknowledge your gift. We will need the donor’s name, the name of the stock, # shares, and the amount. This will make it easier to receive and track the gift.

Wire Transfers

To make wire transfers, please download this form. Email us if you intend to transfer stock to our account so we may acknowledge your gift. Please include the donor’s name and the amount of the transfer.

Matching Gifts

Take advantage of your employer’s matching gifts program. Please help us by adding us to your company’s matching gift program! Please email us if you would like us to contact your company to assist.
